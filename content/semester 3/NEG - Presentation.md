	- can be consulted between visits

## Third Party - Why ii
- arbitrators
	- can dictate an outcome
	- always a settlement
- conciliators
	- enhances communication

## Third Party - When
- emotional or stressful environment
- lack of expertise
- too much ego
- high motivation
- backup tactic
